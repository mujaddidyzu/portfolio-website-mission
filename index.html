My Mission in 2022

Mission

The overall aim of the AMR Innovation Mission Sweden 2022 is to help the global curbing of Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) by boosting international collaboration and joint innovation. The Mission aims to connect the Swedish AMR ecosystem to the international AMR ecosystem and vice versa.

Vision

VISION 2022 is our pledge to create value that contributes to social well-being by leveraging the diversity and strength of our products and technologies in the Automotive, Infocommunications, Electronics, Environment & Energy, and Industrial Materials segments.
